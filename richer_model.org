#+title: richer-model

One of my general preferences when developing systems is to keep the
models relatively rich. This does /not/ translate to something like
die-hard application of object definition, but fairly directly aligns
with the Rule of Representation - data seems easier to reason about
that logic, and weakly defined data tends to breed a proliferation of
logic to deal with it.

This therefore translates to trying to represent things in logic
rather than data, and where there's repetition introducing some form
of model.

I don't want to have to remember that something means something...I
want it to just tell me.
