#+title: dictionary

A dictionary represents a structure where values are associated with
keys. This includes what may also be called associative arrays, maps,
or (the most implementation biased) hashes.

These have the property that the values associated with the keys can
be retrieved and optionally set, and such operations are likely to be
optimized (an order of growth along the lines of of constant or
logarithmic should be available where appropriate).
