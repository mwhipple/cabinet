<h3 class="heading" id="LilyPond-1"><span>LilyPond<a class="copiable-link" href="#LilyPond-1"> &para;</a></span></h3>

<p>... music notation for everyone
</p>
</div>

<div id="lilylogo">
  <img src="pictures/double-lily-modified3.png" alt="LilyPond logo">
</div>

<div id="quickSummary">
<p>LilyPond is a music engraving program, devoted to producing the
highest-quality sheet music possible.  It brings the aesthetics of
traditionally engraved music to computer printouts.  LilyPond is free
software and part of the <a class="uref" href="https://gnu.org">GNU Project</a>.
</p>

<div class="align-right">
<p>Read more in our <a class="ref" href="introduction.html">Introduction</a>!
</p>

</div>

</div>


<div id="homepage-main">

<div class="column-center-top">

<h4 class="subheading" id="Beautiful-Sheet-Music"><span>Beautiful Sheet Music<a class="copiable-link" href="#Beautiful-Sheet-Music"> &para;</a></span></h4>

<div id="homepage-example-image-wrapper">

  </a>

<p>LilyPond is a powerful and flexible tool for engraving tasks of
all kinds, for example
<a class="uref" href="examples.html#Classical-Music">classical music</a>
(like the example above by J.S. Bach),
<a class="uref" href="examples.html#Complex-Notation">complex notation</a>,
<a class="uref" href="examples.html#Early-Music">early music</a>,
<a class="uref" href="examples.html#Modern-Music">modern music</a>,
<a class="uref" href="examples.html#Tablature">tablature</a>,
<a class="uref" href="examples.html#Vocal-Music">vocal music</a>,
<a class="uref" href="examples.html#Lead-Sheets">lead sheets</a>,
<a class="uref" href="examples.html#Educational-Applications">educational materials</a>,
<a class="uref" href="examples.html#Large-Projects">large orchestral projects</a>,
<a class="uref" href="examples.html#Customized-Output">customized output</a>,
and even
<a class="uref" href="examples.html#Schenker-Graphs">Schenker graphs</a>.
</p>

<p>Browse our gallery of <a class="ref" href="examples.html">Examples</a> and be inspired!
